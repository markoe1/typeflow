Introduction

Information We Collect

Local Storage Data

TypeFlow stores certain information locally on your device using your browser's localStorage feature. This information includes:

  • Theme Preferences: Your selected visual theme (Dark, Light, or Retro)
  • Lesson Progress: Your completed lessons and accuracy scores
  • Settings: Your preferred difficulty levels and test configurations

This data is stored only on your local device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or using your browser's privacy settings.

Usage Analytics

We may collect non-personally identifiable information about your use of our Service, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type and browser information
  • Referring website information

This information is used solely to understand how users interact with our platform and to improve our services.

Information We Do NOT Collect

TypeFlow does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • User accounts or registration data
  • The actual text you type during exercises
  • Keystroke logging or detailed typing behavior
  • Payment or financial information
